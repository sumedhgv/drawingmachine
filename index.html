Planet Generator: Something that has always fascinated me was the vastness of space and how countless plants and stars are scattered all across the known universe. Technology has come a long way in allowing us to understand just how big the universe is and what it may contain but one thing we still cannot do is explore these unreachable worlds. So to us, they dont even exist. With the planet generator we can fill that vastness and emptiness of space as it exists in our minds and allow us to imagine space as a place filled with planets, some that are safe for us to explore and others not so much. /h1>
